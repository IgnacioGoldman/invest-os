# 👁️ Eye · 🧠 Brain · ✋ Hand · 🛡️ Shield

## The Investment Operating System

A unified platform that helps investors **see, understand, execute, and protect** their investments across stocks and crypto.

> **See everything. Understand everything. Act from one place.**

# 💰 Business Models & Realistic Outcomes

## Scenario A — Brain Credits (Consumer)

### Conservative

* 5,000 users
* 15% monthly active Brain users
* Average spend: €8/month

```text
5,000 × 15% × €8
= €6,000/month
= €72,000/year
```

### Realistic

* 20,000 users
* 20% monthly active Brain users
* Average spend: €10/month

```text
20,000 × 20% × €10
= €40,000/month
= €480,000/year
```

### Strong Execution

* 100,000 users
* 20% monthly active Brain users
* Average spend: €12/month

```text
100,000 × 20% × €12
= €240,000/month
= €2.88M/year
```

# ⚠️ Biggest Risks

1. Brokerage & exchange integrations.
2. Cost basis and tax calculations.
3. Regulatory requirements around investment recommendations.
4. User acquisition in a crowded fintech market.

---

# 🔑 Core Insight

The moat is not AI.

The moat is becoming the **single source of truth for a user's entire investment life**:

* Stocks
* Crypto
* Transactions
* Cost basis
* Taxes
* Performance
* Risk

Once users connect all their accounts, the platform gains the context needed to deliver highly personalized investment intelligence.
